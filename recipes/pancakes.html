Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk and Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stire to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan.
  4. Cook for two or three minutes, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until both sides are brown.
